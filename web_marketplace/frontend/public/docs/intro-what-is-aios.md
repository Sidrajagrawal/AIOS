# What is AI-OS?
<p className="font-hand text-xl text-gray-400 mb-8">The intelligence-first operating layer.</p>

---

AI-OS is an intelligent operating layer designed to sit on top of your existing Linux environment. Rather than replacing your traditional kernel, AI-OS introduces a **multi-agent orchestrator** capable of executing complex workflows natively on your machine.

By integrating specialized digital experts (Agents), AI-OS transforms your desktop into an autonomous workspace.

## Core Philosophy
1. **Intelligence First:** Every interaction is processed through an LLM-driven brain to determine the optimal execution path.
2. **Modular Capabilities:** Functionality is distributed across isolated, specialized agents rather than a monolithic codebase.
3. **Native Execution:** Agents have securely bridged access to your local filesystem, CLI, and window management tools.
